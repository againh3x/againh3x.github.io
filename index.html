Mission Statement

PublicForumAI is dedicated to making public forum debate more accessible. We provide resources and guidance to help debaters of all levels improve, especially those without access to traditional support. Our goal is to equip students with the skills and confidence to succeed in debate.

AI System FAQs

How does the AI work?

Our hybrid architecture combines several large language models and web search APIs that are fed example cases and combined through natural language processing techniques.

Is This Cheating?

No. Our AI tools are not meant to be used in real rounds, and will not generate links to sources in rebuttal and summary for this reason. PublicForumAI is meant to help users prepare for rounds through real debate experience that can be accessed without human opponents from anywhere at anytime.

Can I recieve feedback after my round?

Yes, you will recieve structured AI feedback and a chosen "winner" upon completing a full practice round. You also have the option to choose between a tech and a lay judge, though we often find the feedback from the AI tech judge to be more helpful.

Is there a crossfire feature?

While it is definitely possible, we do not yet have a crossfire feature that would be fast enough to replicate a real round. However, its in the works!

How long will a round take?

The full practice round feature takes roughly the same amount of time as any PF round, just without crossfire. So, around 35 minutes with prep time.

What if I'm new to debate and not good enough to debate the AI?

We offer features meant to help debaters of all levels improve, including our new2debate and our middle school and high school levels.

Our Journey

Beginning in early 2024, during my sophomore year, it started as a basic script with countless flaws and plenty of room for improvement. It then became part of a computer science class project, and I made slight improvements. However, as the months went on, I worked on significantly improving the code and the "PF structure."" By March 2025, I made major improvements to the website, enhanced the algorithm, and greatly boosted its overall quality. I partnered with another student on Horace Mann's debate team familiar with entrepereneurship to significanty increase accessibility and promote PublicForumAI to debaters around the country. Now, we plan to continue making improvements and focus on increasing our reach.

About Our Tools at PublicForumAI

AI Flow

Pick the speech, and use voice recognition to flow debates automatically with PF structure, helping you track arguments and improve round strategy.

AI Practice Round

Engage in a FULL PF DEBATE AGAINST AN AI! Record your speeches while the AI flows and generates responses in real-time, following the standard PF format. You can choose the level of difficulty, and RECEIVE DETAILED FEEDBACK with results at the end of rounds, highlighting your strengths and areas for improvement!

AI Casewriter

Instantly generate structured cases with evidence for any topic and side, teaching you how to craft stronger arguments and contentions. We offer both a middle school and high school feature.
